# Derived Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Unit |
|--------|---------|------|
| Capacity Factor | Annual Yield / (Capacity × 8760) | dimensionless |
| Performance Ratio | Yield / (GHI × Area × Efficiency) | dimensionless |
| Specific Yield | Annual Yield / Capacity | kWh/kWp |
| P90 Yield | 10th percentile of MC distribution | kWh |
